# ICP: Bookkeeper / Controller

## Who they are
- Bookkeepers, controllers, finance ops at small-to-mid businesses
- Industries: same as SMB owner
- Personal: 30–50, often the person who actually USES accounting software daily

## What they care about
- Hours back in their week
- Reducing manual data entry
- Reconciliation accuracy

## What they don't care about
- High-level strategy decks (they're operators, not strategists)
- Pitches focused on the CEO's pain (they have their own pain)

## Buying signals
- Asking in communities about workflow automation
- Posting frustration with current tools
- Recently expanded the team (more transactions = more work)

## Anti-signals
- Already using competitor X (require switching cost analysis before pitching)

## Where to find them
- LinkedIn (with title filter)
- Bookkeeping community forums
- Webinars / training events
